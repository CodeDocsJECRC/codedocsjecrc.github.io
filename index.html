About CodeDocs

CodeDocs is a community of students around the college, intended for learning , building , competing and contributing to open source, networking and collaborations with industry experts in the tech world.


Great software is more than code. At CodeDocs we'll help you build best in industry software applications, how people work together to solve hard problems of developing software.


Build: How do you decide what to build and how to build it? Discuss with colleagues, hear from experts, their thought process before writing a code.


Collaborate: Software takes a team. Learn how engineers work together to overcome technical challenges and efficiently build software.


Deploy: We'll discuss how to maintain quality, ensure availability, collect feedback, and keep building products people love and incubate to launch them.
